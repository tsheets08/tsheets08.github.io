About

Texas Team Handball is starting up a league in the Dallas area this winter. This will be the inaugural year for the TTHBL and we want to gain interest and involvement with the people around North Texas! We are in the works with a predominate site for this league and will be bringing updates accordingly. Spread the word! Please call or email for any questions.
